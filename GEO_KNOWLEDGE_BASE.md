## GEO vs SEO

| Aspect | SEO (Traditional) | GEO (AI-first) |
|--------|------------------|----------------|
| **Goal** | Rank #1 in SERPs | Be cited by AI in 2-7 source answers |
| **Focus** | Keywords + backlinks | Entities + citations + structured data |
| **Query type** | "best CRM software" | "What CRM for a startup with 10 people?" |
| **Content style** | Keyword-optimized | Concise, structured, FAQ-style |
| **Trust signals** | Backlinks, DA | E-E-A-T, reviews, earned media |
| **Discovery channel** | Google, Bing | ChatGPT, SGE, Perplexity, Claude, Copilot |
| **Metrics** | Clicks, rankings, CTR | AI citation frequency, referral traffic |
| **Feedback loop** | Months | Days to weeks |

## 4-Phase GEO Framework

1. **ASSESS** — baseline AI search readiness; are AI engines citing you at all?
2. **OPTIMIZE** — structure content, build entity authority, nail technical foundations
3. **MEASURE** — track AI citation frequency, share of voice, sentiment
4. **ITERATE** — scale what earns citations; identify which AI platforms drive most value

## Vertical: Law Firms

### Common Gaps
- No JSON-LD/Schema.org markup (primary GEO lever for this segment)
- HTTP 403 blocking AI bots (GPTBot, ClaudeBot) — very common
- No FAQ section — LLMs lose Q&A structure
- Missing or incomplete sitemap.xml
- Thin content on homepage

### GEO Levers
- FAQ schema + FAQ content sections
- Organization schema, LocalBusiness schema, LegalService schema
- Author bio pages with credentials (E-E-A-T)
- Robot.txt audit to unblock AI crawlers
- Fresh, citation-worthy content (statutes, case references)

---

## Vertical: DTC / eCommerce

### Common Gaps
- Missing Product schema markup
- No FAQ section on product pages
- Outdated product data / pricing
- Weak review integration
- Conversational content gap — keyword-heavy, not intent-focused

### GEO Levers
- Product JSON-LD schema (price, availability, reviews)
- FAQ section on every product page answering real customer questions
- Review schema + photo reviews
- Intent-first content: answer "what is it for?" not just "what is it?"
- Connect to Google Merchant Center
